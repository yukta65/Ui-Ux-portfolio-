Case Study

TrackAcademy
Student Attendance & Progress System

Mobile App EdTech Flutter Multi-user
View Figma Prototype

📋 Project Overview

TrackAcademy is a comprehensive mobile application built with Flutter that bridges the communication gap between students, teachers, and parents. The app provides real-time attendance tracking, academic progress monitoring, and seamless communication channels.

My Role: Lead UI/UX Designer | Duration: 6 weeks | Platform: iOS & Android

🎯 Problem Statement

Schools struggle with manual attendance systems, delayed parent communication, and fragmented academic tracking. Parents want real-time updates about their child's attendance and performance, while teachers need efficient tools to manage multiple classes.

🔍 Research & Discovery

I conducted interviews with 15 teachers and 20 parents to understand pain points:

  • 78% of parents wanted instant attendance notifications
  • Teachers spent 15-20 minutes per class on manual attendance
  • 82% of parents felt uninformed about their child's daily progress
  • Communication delays led to missed academic interventions

💡 Design Solution

I designed a three-portal system with role-based dashboards:

  • Student App: View attendance, check grades, access study materials, submit assignments
  • Teacher Portal: Mark attendance with QR codes, grade assignments, communicate with parents
  • Parent Dashboard: Real-time attendance alerts, progress reports, direct teacher messaging

🎨 Key Features Designed

  • Smart Attendance: QR code-based check-in system with geofencing
  • Live Dashboard: Real-time updates on attendance percentage and trends
  • Progress Tracking: Visual grade reports with subject-wise breakdowns
  • Direct Messaging: In-app communication between teachers and parents
  • Resource Library: Study materials, notes, and assignment uploads

✅ Results & Impact

  • Reduced attendance marking time by 85% (from 15 min to 2 min per class)
  • Parents receive notifications within 30 seconds of attendance marking
  • Teacher-parent communication response time improved by 70%
  • Successfully tested with 200+ beta users across 3 schools

🚀 Technologies & Tools

Figma for UI/UX design • Flutter for cross-platform development • Firebase for real-time sync • User testing with Maze

Case Study

TrackKaro
Real-Time School Bus Tracking

Mobile AppGPS TrackingSafetyReal-time
View Figma Prototype

📋 Project Overview

TrackKaro is a GPS-enabled mobile application designed to give parents peace of mind by providing real-time tracking of school buses. Parents receive instant notifications when their child boards the bus, during the journey, and upon safe arrival at school or home.

My Role: UI/UX Designer & Researcher | Duration: 4 weeks | Platform: iOS & Android

🎯 Problem Statement

Parents constantly worry about their children's safety during school commutes. Delays, route changes, and lack of real-time information create anxiety. Schools struggle with manual bus attendance and lack efficient communication tools for transportation updates.

🔍 User Research Insights

Surveyed 50 parents and interviewed 10 school transport coordinators:

  • 92% of parents expressed anxiety about not knowing bus location
  • Average parent makes 3-4 phone calls daily to check bus status
  • 65% of parents reported late pickups without prior notification
  • Schools lacked digital systems for bus attendance verification

💡 Design Solution

  • Live Map View: Real-time GPS tracking with route visualization
  • Smart Notifications: Automated alerts for boarding, en-route milestones, and arrival
  • Safety Features: SOS button, route deviation alerts, and driver verification
  • History Tracking: Complete journey logs with timestamps

✅ Impact & Results

  • Eliminated 90% of parent phone calls to schools about bus status
  • Reduced parent anxiety scores by 78% (measured via post-launch survey)
  • Bus attendance verification improved from 70% to 98% accuracy
  • Average boarding notification delivered in under 30 seconds

🚀 Design Tools Used

Figma for design system • Google Maps API integration planning • User journey mapping with Miro • Prototype testing with UserTesting.com

📋 Project Overview

Designed a comprehensive e-commerce website for a clothing retailer, focusing on desktop shopping experience. The project included complete information architecture, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups for all key user flows from browsing to checkout.

My Role: UI/UX Designer | Duration: 5 weeks | Type: Wireframe & UI Design

🎯 Design Challenge

Create an intuitive desktop shopping experience that helps users discover products quickly, compare options easily, and complete purchases with confidence.

🔍 Competitive Analysis

  • Most sites had cluttered filtering systems with 10+ options
  • Product images lacked consistent quality and zoom functionality
  • Average cart abandonment rate was 68% for fashion websites
  • Users wanted better size guides and fit recommendations

🎨 Key Screens Designed

  • Homepage: Hero banner, category navigation, trending products
  • Product Listing: Grid/list view toggle, multi-level filters, sorting
  • Product Detail: Image gallery, size guide, reviews, similar products
  • Shopping Cart: Saved items, promo codes, shipping calculator
  • Checkout Flow: Guest/member checkout, address validation, payment options

🚀 Tools & Methods

Figma for wireframing & UI design • Miro for user journey mapping • FigJam for brainstorming • Competitive analysis frameworks
